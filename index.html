Secret Message Generator Welcome to a simple secrete message generator app. This little app lets you encode your text message. Such that you could send it to a friend who knows how to decode it, or challenge your friends by letting them find out for themselves. Currently there is only one alphabet shift encoder and I do not plan to add new encoders, but if you are interested in doing so, send me a pull request. I'll be happy to add your new encoders. Alphabet shift This encoder will shift each letter of the message by the shift you desire. Only alphabetic letters a to z are encoded, all other letters will not be changed.
