Secret Message Generator Welcome to a simple secrete text generator app. This little app lets you encode your text message. Such that you could sent it to an friend how knows how to decode it or you could also challenge your friends by letting them figure it out themselves. Currently there is only one alphabet shift encoder. I do not plan to add new encoders, but if anyone is interested in doing so, they can send me a pull request. I'll be happy to add new encoders. Alphabet shift This encoder will shift each letter of the message by the shift you desire. Only alphabetic letters a to z are encoded, all other letters will be ignored.
